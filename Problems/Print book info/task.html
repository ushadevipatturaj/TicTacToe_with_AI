Print book info Open on JetBrains Academy

Your task is to write a function print_book_info(title, author=None, year=None) that prints information about a book. Arguments author and year are optional, so be ready that they might equal to None.

You don't have to read the input. The information about a book will be passed to your function, and it should output it in the right format.

See the samples below to understand the output format.

The knowledge of if-elif-else statements is expected in this task. If it's not the case, please proceed to the next challenge.


Sample Input:
title: War and Peace
author: Leo Tolstoy
year: 1869
Sample Output:
"War and Peace" was written by Leo Tolstoy in 1869

Sample Input:
title: Crime and Punishment
author: None
year: 1866
Sample Output:
"Crime and Punishment" was written in 1866

Sample Input:
title: The Chronicles of Narnia
author: C. S. Lewis
year: None
Sample Output:
"The Chronicles of Narnia" was written by C. S. Lewis

Sample Input:
title: Harry Potter
author: None
year: None
Sample Output:
"Harry Potter"
